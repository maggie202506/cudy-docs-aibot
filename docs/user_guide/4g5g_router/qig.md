# Quick Installation Guide

Follow the instructions below to get your router ready for use fast and easy.

<img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/qig.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

1. Insert your nano SlM card into the SIM slot (on the bottom) according to the engraved direction, until you hear a clicking sound.

2. Plug the power adapter to the router, and wait for 2~5 minutes until the system LED turns solid on.

3. Check the Internet LED.
    - If the Internet LED is in yellow or white, the router is already connected to the cellular network.
    - If the Internet LED is in red, please continue with the following steps to configure the router for Ethernet network.

4. Connect to the router through Wi-Fi or with an Ethernet cable.
    - Through WiFi: Connect your device to the router's Wi-Fi using the SSlD (network name) and Password printed on the bottom of the router.
    - With an Ethernet cable: Turn off the Wi-Fi on your device and connect it to the router's LAN port with an Ethernet cable.
        <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/ethernet-connection.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

5. Launch a browser, enter *[cudy.net](www.cudy.net)* (or *[192.168.10.1](192.168.10.1)*) in the address bar, and create an administrator password to log in.

6. Follow the step-by-step instructions to finish the *Quick Setup*.
    - Select your *Time Zone*. Click *Next*.
        <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/qs1.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
    - Select your *APN Profile* (*Auto* recommended). For other options, check with your SIM card provider. Click *Next*.
        <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/qs2.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
    - Customize your 2.4G/5G Wireless SSID and password or just keep it as default. Click *Next*.
        <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/qs3.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />
    - Confirm your settings or click *Back* to make adjustments. Click *Save and Apply*.
        <img src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/Cudytech-pr/User-Guide/docs/images/4g5g_router/qs4.webp" alt="" width="500px" style="display: block; margin: 0 auto;" />

!!! Note
    - If the signal is still too weak indoors, try installing your extension antennas (not included in the box) to the SMA connector and placing the antennas outdoors.
    - If a *SIM blocked* window pops up, please input your SIM PIN or contact your carrier for help.
